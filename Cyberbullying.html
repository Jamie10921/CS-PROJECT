DEFINITION OF
CYBERBULLYING
-----------------------------
cyber + bullying
meaning of computers or computer networks
mistreating someone weaker than oneself


Cyberbullying is bullying using computers or the internet.


EXAMPLES OF
CYBERBULLYING
-----------------------------
Sending mean messages to someone or posting mean things about them online.


Spreading rumors about others
Posting embrassing photos or videos of someone online


Impersonating someone by making a fake profile of them






EFFECTS OF
CYBERBULLYING
-----------------------------
Cyberbullying causes the victim to experience psychological and emotional distress.


The victim might not feel safe anywhere because they can’t use their computer or the internet without seeing someone’s hurtful comments.




Their self-esteem and self-confidence will get affected, and they might feel worthless and powerless.


They might become isolated and ostracized from their peers.






IF YOU'RE BEING
CYBERBULLIED
-----------------------------
  1. Don't try to retaliate or get revenge because that might make the situation worse.
  2. Keep evidence by screenshotting instances of the cyberbullying.
  3. Tell an adult you trust, like a parent or teacher, about the cyberbullying.
  4. Take a break off of social media for awhile.
