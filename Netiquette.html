DEFINITION OF
NETIQUETTE
-----------------------------
net + etiquette
refering to the internet, a global computer network
rules about observing proper manners and behavior




SIGNIFICANCE OF
NETIQUETTE
-----------------------------
When communicating online, nonverbal communication, like
their facial expression or their voice's tone, are limited, which
might led to miscommunication. By practicing proper
netiquette, people are able to communicate more efficiently.




SOME RULES OF
NETIQUETTE
-----------------------------
Don't speak in ALL-CAPS, especially in business situations, because it makes you look angry. Also during business / professional situations, use correct grammar, punctuation, and spelling because that shows professionalism.


If you use emojis, use the appropriate emojis the situation. Using the wrong emojis in the wrong situation will create misunderstandings.

Don't say inappropriate or rude things because that might offend others.



Remember that even though you are anonymous online, it doesn't mean that you should post harmful or mean things because you can.





In general, you should treat people online with respect,
like how you treat people in real life.
