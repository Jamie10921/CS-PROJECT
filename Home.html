Imagine a world, where Internet doesn't exist.

Can't?
That's because it's hard imagining a world without everyday convenience at your fingertips.

Social Media (singular: Social Medium) are platforms/programs that can create interactions among people that use these media platforms. It usually contains some user-generated content, some of which are texts, comments, posted photos and videos. These platforms collect data from their users in order to deliver personal services (such as advertisements, recommended activities, etc.)


Most of us want to be known. How do we do that? By two methods. One can be through social interactions with your friends, or other people. The other can be through social media. We post our thoughts, pictures, videos and others, for our friends (or even the world!) to see. But not all of us get noticed. We tend to feel sad that we see our friends' pictures get more likes than our pictures. Because of Social Media, people often compare themselves to what they see and hear online.

Whether you're scrolling through your feed on Facebook, tweeting on Twitter, posting on Instagram, or even streaming on YouTube, you're using the Internet!
